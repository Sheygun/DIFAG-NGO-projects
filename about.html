About Us

BACKGROUND

Today’s Africa is characterized by mass poverty; unemployment; low productivity; huge debt burden; illiteracy; poor health service delivery system; high morbidity/mortality rate, injustice, corruption, etc. Though nature has endowed Africa with abundant resources, both human and material, it is an irony that Africa is still grossly under-developed and experiencing enormous poverty.

While efforts by Ministries, Departments and Agencies (MDAs) as well as philanthropists have contributed toward reduction in the suffering of the masses, they appear not to be enough as evidenced by the current socio-economic crises facing individuals, nations and the continent at large. It is for this reason that non-state actors such as Development Initiative for Africa Growth (DIFAG) must be fully involved in the campaign for innovative activities that would ameliorate the anti-growth issues in Africa’s development.

WHO WE ARE

The Development Initiative for Africa Growth (DIFAG) is an international Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) that is non-political and non-profit-making which seeks to promote sustainable development in Africa through integrated development programmes that empower individuals to be useful to themselves and the society.

DIFAG came into existence in March, 2003 to contribute to the growth of African continent by working with Africans in ensuring that they fully realise their potentials and talents in the service to their communities.

The primary constituency of DIFAG is the people in urban and rural communities in the belief that development is possible, feasible and empowering, if it is people-centered, educational, infrastructural and service oriented.

VISION STATEMENT

DIFAG’s vision is to transform the African continent to a land where there is political emancipation (equality, freedom, good governance, transparency and accountability); justice, peace, security and general well-being.

-- Development Initiative for Africa Growth (DIFAG)

MISSION STATEMENT

To empower Africans to discover and build their potentials and significantly increase their well-being in all spheres of life.

-- Development Initiative for Africa Growth (DIFAG)

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

DIFAG aims, among others, to:

1.

ensure full grooming and blossoming of youths so that they can grow into responsible adults for future leadership;

2.

promote human development through integrated development programs;

3.

develop the mechanism through which interactions among African entrepreneurs can be promoted;

4.

contribute to the realization of people’s full potentials and appreciation of their worth and dignity;

5.

support and promote education, training and skills acquisition;

6.

promote positive attitudes in Africans;

7.

collect, collate, analyze and disseminate relevant information to stakeholders; and

8.

collaborate and cooperate with similar organizations across the globe.
