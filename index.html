    • Welcome to DIFAG


    The Development Initiative for Africa Growth (DIFAG) is a registered international Non-Governmental Organisation (NGO) that is non-political and non-profit-making which seeks to promote sustainable development in Africa through integrated development programmes that empower individuals to be useful to themselves and the society.

    Know More



    How We Started

    DIFAG came into existence in 2003 to contribute to the growth of African Continent by working with Africans in ensuring that they fully realise their potentials and talents in the service to their communities.

    What We Believe

    The primary constituency of DIFAG is the people in urban and rural communities in the belief that development is possible, feasible and empowering, if it is people-centred, educational, infrastructural and service oriented.
